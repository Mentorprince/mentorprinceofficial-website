About Us

Tech Skils Facilitator | Educator

We deliver top-notch online and offline training on tech skills.
Experts in making your tech dreams come to pass
with testimonies from our clients across the globe.

Think Digital Skills Network is an initiative established to promote IT literacy across Afica. Our goal is to ensure teenagers and adults in African Continent are well exposed to IT and the benefits that accompanies it. We are determined to train our clients, promote thier skills and ensure they have adequate experience, and knowledege to leverage their employability and career growth.

Our headquater is based at 12 Mubarak Estate, Alagbole - Akute Road, Ojodu Berger, Lagos Nigeria. We operate online with over 5,000 students trained and equipped with tech skills and facilities to enable them thrive in the tech world.

REQUIREMENTS

At least a basic education at secondary level is required to join the team of those making technological impact around the globe.

TARGET/GOAL

To train, equip and connect youngsters and the working class to leverage their employability and career growth within the shortest possible time.
